Grilled Cheese

Description

This gooey but still crispy grilled cheese is one for the record books! The sourdough bread gets a nice crunch while still allowing the bread to transfer heat to melt the glorious cheese inside. The different cheeses in combination with each other make a new mouthfeel that leaves the tastebuds begging for more.

With this easy recipe you will be able to have a childhood classic in a more elevated time in your life. Remember when you thought being a grown-up was going to be filled with non-stop yummy food, and adventures with your freinds? Well in about 6 minutes this recipe can help you with the yummy food part, and make you forget that you don't have any friends anymore..

Ingredients

serves 2

  • 4 slices of Sourdough Bread
  • 4 slices of Sharp Cheddar
  • 4 slices of Munster
  • 2 slices of American Cheese
  • favorate butter(mine is garlic parm butter)

Steps

  1. Get the pan on the stove at a low/med temp. while getting the ingredients out on the cutting board.
  2. Put a generous amount of butter on one side of each slice of bread.
  3. Once the pan is up to temperature, place two slices of bread butter side down in the pan.
  4. Put 2 slices of Cheddar on each slice of bread,do the same for 2 slices of Munster, and 1 slice of American
  5. top the 2 pile of cheeses with the remaining slices of bread, butter-side up. Cover pan with a lid if possible, if not, lower the temperature.
  6. Once the bottom of the sandwhich is getting golden brown, flip it over and press the sandwhich down to the pan. The sourdough is a tough enough bread that it is not going to get crushed and lose it's structure like a slices of brioche would.
  7. Cook until desired crispyness and where the cheese is gooey
  8. enjoy
